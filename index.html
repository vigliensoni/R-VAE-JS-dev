This is R-VAE-JS

a rhythmic model player and visualizer for R-VAE

Press Go! to load a model and play

GO!

Instructions:

1. Click to retrieve and decode a rhythm

2. Drag the mouse to interpolate rhythms

3. Change the Threshold to modify the number of onsets retrieved

4. Change the Noise to add variability to mouse position

5. Mute individuals instruments by pressing Q, W, or E

6. Mute all by pressing R
